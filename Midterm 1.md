3 groups were slated for total annihilation:

## Exclusion to Extermination

Genocide was not an immediate action but a radicalizing process: 

Initial Phase was focused on legal discrimination like the Nuremburg Laws, IE forced sterilization and encouraged emigration.

Then it moved towards the Blueprint Operation T-4: the secret killing of disabled people provided the technology and blueprint for the wider Holocaust. IE gas chambers disguised as showers.

Radicalization hit during the war: With the invasion of the Soviet Union Shifted policy from resettlement/emigration to systematic slaughter. (The Final Solution.)

- Dehumanization: Victims were stripped of their identity through tattooing, head-shaving, and horrific living conditions in ghettos and labor camps. 

For the Jewish people they faced Systemic Extermination
